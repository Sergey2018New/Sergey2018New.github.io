Barge transport

Contact Us

Arctic Heavylift Ltd. is well experienced with project cargo transportation on the barges across Russian inland waterways.

We are in touch with all barge carriers, operating on Russian rivers and know all aspects of working with them. By order of the client we can arrange mobilisation of the barges of all the types (including ice-classed barges) from other regions by tugs, heavy-lift or semi-submersible vessels.

Arctic Heavylift Ltd. is the agent of leading marine design bureaus in Russia and can arrange barge transportation projects development and approval in the Register of Shipping.
